How SecretBid Works

Four cryptographic phases keep bids sealed on-chain — no trust required, no off-chain servers, no front-running.

Phase 01
Commit Your Bid

Submit keccak256(amount, nonce, address) on-chain. Your actual bid stays completely hidden until the reveal window opens. A deposit locks your position.

On-Chainkeccak256Deposit
Phase 02
Reveal Phase

After bidding closes, submit your plaintext amount + nonce. The contract verifies your hash matches. Invalid or missing reveals forfeit your deposit.

Verify HashHighest WinsNonce Key
Phase 03
REX Privacy Relay

Route your transactions through the REX relay to mask your identity. Zero-knowledge proofs verify bid validity without exposing your on-chain address.

Phase 04
Auto Settlement

Winner pays the winning amount and receives the RWA. All losing bidders get full deposits refunded automatically. Excess deposit returned instantly.

Zero-Knowledge Privacy

Your Bid Is a Secret Until You Reveal It

SecretBid's commit-reveal scheme means your bid amount is never visible during the auction. The REX relay adds an identity layer — keeping your strategy entirely private.

  • Hash commitment locks bid on-chain without revealing the amount
  • ETH deposit proves intent — forfeited only on missing or invalid reveal
  • ZK proofs verify bids without exposing wallet address to anyone
  • REX relay masks transaction origin at the network level
  • No off-chain servers — every action lives entirely on Sepolia

Why SecretBid

SecretBid never holds your RWAs or ETH in a centralized vault. The smart contract is the escrow — auditable, immutable, publicly verifiable on Sepolia.
